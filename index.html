PURPOSE

We deserve a better academic experience.
No student should suffer in silence.
We deserve to be seen not just as students but as humans with dreams, talents,struggles, and potential.
We do not just want to survive Veterinary Medicine, We want to live through it, thrive in it, and leave it better than we met it.

I believe we can rebrand AVMS and make history. Our Alumnis are recognised among the top in the country. We do not have to wait till we graduate to be recognised, we can shine as undergraduates of Veterinary Medicine. Veterinary Students are more than just brains, we have all it takes to make things happen

VISION

To build a faculty where students do not just pursue Veterinary Medicine but live it with pride, wellness, and purpose of strong minds, united individuals and a network that thrives beyond graduation.

MISSION

To foster an inclusive , mentally supportive, and academically empowering faculty where every Veterinary student is seen, heard and uplifted by creating sustainable welfare structures, promoting unity through impactful engagement, and pioneering student-led innovations that leave a lasting legacy.

MY BELIEF

  • I believe no student should lose themselves in the pursuit of their dream.
  • I believe true Leadership is about active representation, standing for students, walking with them, and carrying their needs until they are met
  • I believe in Unity over division, n building a system where we support each other academically, socially, spiritually, and professionally.
  • I believe our struggles can shape us but only when we walk through them together.
  • I believe a student body, when empowered, can change it's own story and I am here to lead that change.
